Privacy Policy

Spoken Inc is committed to protecting your privacy when you use our website and app. This privacy policy explains:
• the information we collect when you visit our website and use our app,
• how we collect it,
• how we use it, and
• how we protect it.
This Policy does not apply to offline activities.
Anonymous Information
Spoken Inc may collect anonymous information about users who visit our site and aggregate this anonymous information for statistical analysis. For instance, we may use it to determine usage patterns so we know which parts of our site are most popular. Examples of the type of anonymous information we collect and how we collect it are:
Internet Tags: provide information about your browser (e.g., Microsoft Internet Explorer®, Firefox®, Google Chrome®), operating system (e.g., Windows, OSX), Internet service provider, geolocation and similar information.
IP Addresses: a number assigned to your computer by a Web server when you are on the Internet; we might use IP address information to determine the number of users who have visited a specific area of our site. Please note we could use this information to identify you if necessary to comply with legal requirements or for security issues.
Personally Identifiable Information
What is Personally Identifiable Information? Personally Identifiable Information (“Your Information”) includes information such as your name, email address, company name, telephone number and copies of communications shared through our contact forms or post comments. If you purchase our application through the Apple App Store, your credit card information is stored securely on that partner’s system, and we have no access to your financial data save for the information about the actual purchase (what was purchased, when it was purchased, amount spent and the shipping details).
How We Collect Your Information. Spoken Inc collects Your Information only when you voluntarily provide it to us on our site by entering your email address to receive updates and information from Spoken Inc. In the future we may add other instruments to our website to include contact forms, user forums, polls or surveys.
You may need to provide Your Information to participate in certain Spoken Inc programs and activities or receive certain services or offers. Any actual purchases offered on our site involve providing Your Information to a third-party service provider, in this case, Apple via the App Store. In these circumstances, you will know that you are on another website because the URL will change. You should read the privacy policy of the third-party site before providing Your Information.
When an individual enters their email address into the Spoken Inc site, Google will automatically transfer that email to the Spoken Inc email subscription list. Emails will then be sent out to all email addresses on the list to inform the individuals of new products or information provided by Spoken Inc.
How We Use Your Information. Spoken Inc will use Your Information for the purpose for which you give it to us (e.g., to subscribe as a member of the Spoken Inc email list.)
We may use Your Information to send you electronic mailings (“emails”) about Spoken Inc and our services and programs that we think will interest you. While presently not within the scope of our activity, in the future Spoken Inc may also use Your Information to send you occasional electronic mailings about third parties whose information or services we think may be of interest to you.
How We Share Your Information. Spoken Inc may share Your Information with third parties that perform services for Spoken Inc. Spoken Inc also reserves the right to disclose Your Information in circumstances they deem necessary or appropriate, such as to respond to legal process or request from a governmental agency, security reasons or similar situations.
Security. Spoken Inc follows best practices to keep Your Information secure and safeguard it from unauthorized access by using special software tools and firewalls to protect our servers and virus/intrusion/malware detection software. However, no transmission over the Internet can be guaranteed to be 100% secure, so you provide Your Information at your own risk.
Links to Other Websites. Our Site may include links to other Websites that may be of interest to you or are referenced within the body of our content. Any links are provided only as a convenience and do not imply any endorsement of another site. Be sure to read the privacy policies on other sites other Websites.
Transfer to the United States. If you are located outside of the United States, please note that the information you provide to us will be transferred to the United States and that by providing Your Information, you consent to this transfer and storage of the data in the United States.
